2023 Venza

12.3-In. Touchscreen Display With JBL® Premium Sound System

Technology that’s tailored to your needs. With easily customizable screens, Venza’s available 12.3-in. touchscreen display lets you create the perfect setup for every drive.

Hands-Free Power Liftgate

Venza’s advanced technology always seems to be one step ahead of your needs. When your hands are full after running errands, just wave your foot under the rear bumper, and Venza’s standard hands-free power liftgate senses your Smart Key fob and opens automatically.

Five USB Ports

Your choice of chargers awaits. Two charging ports and one media port in the front, plus two additional charging ports in the rear. With USB Type-C and wireless charging options available, you and your passengers can power up your essential devices however you choose.
